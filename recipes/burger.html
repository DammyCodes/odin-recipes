Burger

World Best Burger

These burger patties are made with ground beef and an easy bread crumb mixture. Nothing beats a simple hamburger on a warm summer evening. Pile these burgers with your favorite condiments and pop open a cool drink!

Ingredients

  • Ground Beef
  • Onion
  • Garlic
  • Soy sauce
  • Dried parsley
  • Basil

Steps

  1. Preheat an outdoor grill for high heat and lightly oil the grate.
  2. Combine ground beef, onion, cheese, egg, onion soup mix, minced garlic, garlic powder, soy sauce. Use your hands to form the mixture into 4 patties.
  3. Cook patties on the preheated grill until no longer pink in the center.
